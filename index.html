Marlene and Kenny's

Love Story

  • First Date

    Early 2021

    Marlene and Kenny met in April 2021 online and hit it off right away. As an indicator that defined their relationship their first date which was supposed to be a walk in Roger Williams Park and coffee turned into over nine hours with a full dinner, which set the pace for a relationship that bucked the normal. They knew right away that they had something special.

  • In a Relationship

    May 29, 2021

    Less than 5 months into their relationship they experienced a major catastrophe with Kenny having a life altering car accident. In many relationships this would have been too much, but not for this pair. They knew that they were a team and supported each other. They have weathered many storms after this including months of therapy, job loss as well as the loss of both of Marlene’s parents. Through it all, they persisted, leaning into each other and growing solid in their partnership. In spite of these challenges there was boundless joy, laughter and contentment.

  • Proposal

    April 09, 2022

    For most people they would have been daunted but all of the challenges has made Marlene and Kenny stronger and confident they are each other’s better half. Please join them as they celebrate this unwavering love and commit to each other.
